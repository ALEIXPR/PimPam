Services for schools and teams

We design training and support so pedagogical decisions become clear, shared and sustainable.

We work with schools, staff teams and coordinators who need to organise processes, clarify pedagogical frameworks and turn them into tools that are genuinely useful in everyday practice.

  • Tailor-made training for staff teams, departments and school stages.
  • Design and review of learning situations and assessment approaches.
  • Visual resources to communicate, align and create continuity.

How we help

Three working lines built around real usefulness.

Training

Practical, understandable sessions

We translate curriculum, assessment and active methodologies into examples, decisions and tools teams can use straight away.

Support

Structure without overload

We help schools prioritise, sequence and turn broad goals into concrete, sustainable next steps.

Visual design

Materials that improve alignment

We create visual pieces that make agreements, frameworks and processes easier to understand and share.

What this approach brings

More shared understanding, less fragmentation.

02. We design the intervention
